Unlocking the potential of your holiday home in Austria

23 Jan 2023

·

By Kate Wilkinson

Austria is one of the most beautiful countries in Europe and has a booming tourism industry. Stunning landscapes, rich cultural heritage, delicious food, and world-class ski resorts, results in people from all over the world flocking to this picturesque nation year after year. As a result of its popularity as a tourist destination, there is now an increased demand for holiday homes in Austria. Unlock the potential of your holiday home in Austria with Travelnest.

Holiday Austria TravelNest property near Obertauern

Advertise a holiday home in Austria

Holiday Austria use Travelnest to advertise their impressive selection of properties. With over 30 booking sites available to list their properties on, Holiday Austria are able to provide potential customers competitively priced holiday homes easily managed from one central account. Before getting started with advertising a holiday home, it’s really important to make sure that you comply with local laws surrounding short-term renting in Austria. All short-term rental hosts in Austria are required to maintain a guest register, and hosts should be aware of all the rules and restrictions associated with different cities and states. Vienna, Salzburg, Tyrol and Styria each have different regulations which you can find out more about in our guide.

The advantages of advertising a holiday home

As well as hosting in Austria being so simple, renting a holiday home in Austria has huge potential for earning income due to strong seasonality. Austria is especially attractive for tourism due to its varied range of activities available throughout all four seasons. This makes it popular for both winter and summer holidays. From skiing down the slopes of snow-capped mountains, to taking in the lush countryside views during warmer months, there’s something to suit everyone. Austria boasts some of the finest cuisine and wine regions, making it a great place to relax and enjoy local specialities or unique regional dishes. With an abundance of outdoor activities ranging from lake fishing and hiking to exploring natural parks and forests, visitors can soak up some of nature’s beauty in all its glory. This in turn means your holiday home is attractive to so many different groups of guests, especially those travelling in a large groups. There are many advantages that come with renting a holiday home with high capacity, such as those like Holiday Austria. Sign up for a free Travelnest account today, to join hosts all over Austria who are using our simple platform for generate maximum bookings.
